About Us

At Team Up For Science, we are dedicated to supporting and promoting student participation in robotics, computer science, mathematics, and science competitions. Our mission is to provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities that inspire creativity, critical thinking, and innovation for the next generation of STEM leaders.
